HOW YOU CAN HELP

We all have different strengths to share in the fight to ensure every child in the United States gets the food they need. Whatever your strength, there’s a way you can share it to help kids.

HELP HUNGRY KIDS

Join Team No Kid Hungry by sending in a generous donation. Just $10 can help provide 100 meals for kids.

BRANDS THAT GIVE

Brands across the country are partnering with No Kid Hungry to donate a portion of their sales this back to school season to help kids get the meals they need to thrive in the classroom and in life.

FUNDRAISE

How will you raise money to feed hungry kids? Turn your favorite hobby or personal interest into a fundraiser for No Kid Hungry.

ATTEND AN EVENT

The best chefs in the world are cooking their finest dishes to raise funds and awareness for No Kid Hungry - and you're invited.

SPEAK UP FOR KIDS

Hungry kids don’t have lobbyists. They’re counting on you instead. Write, call and email to urge your elected officials to take a stand for children.

YOUTH ENGAGEMENT

Today’s young people are the generation that will end childhood hunger. If you’re a student looking to get involved, you can join the fight to make No Kid Hungry a reality in your schools and communities.

OTHER WAYS TO GIVE

Interested in donating through your workplace? Want to give a gift of stock or other assets? We can help.
